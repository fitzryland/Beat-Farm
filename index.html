follow us

Beat Farm is an annual camping and dance weekend for our friends and friends of friends.

Come escape the city in an old growth forest with Douglas Firs towering overhead. Dive into the Molalla River with the summer sun reflecting into your eyes. Dance the night away with all of your favorite people. Join us for Beat Farm 2026 at Trout Creek Wilderness Lodge.

Located just one hour south of Portland, in the foothills of the Cascade Mountain Range, the venue includes access to fun and relaxing amenities that make camping in the woods with friends even better. With a private beach for swimming and day time pool parties, a spa area with a hot tub, cold plunge and sauna and various accommodation options to upgrade your camp experience - this is a campout you do not want to miss.

In addition to enjoying all that the Lodge and its beautiful location have to offer, Beat Farm is what we make it. Enjoy pancakes and bacon in the morning, flower crowns and bracelet making in the afternoon, bleach dye your new Beat Farm tank top or get a tarot reading in the evening all while listening to your favorite local DJs.

code of conduct

Beat Farm is committed to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, age, race, ethnicity, nationality, language, or religion. We do not tolerate harassment in any form.

Our definition of “harassment” includes:

  • Offensive comments related to gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, mental illness, neurotype, physical appearance, body, age, race, ethnicity, nationality, language, or religion
  • Unwelcome comments regarding a person’s lifestyle choices and practices, including those related to food, health, parenting, drugs, and employment
  • Physical contact without consent or after a request to stop
  • Threats of violence
  • Incitement of violence towards any individual, including encouraging a person to commit suicide or to engage in self harm
  • Deliberate intimidation
  • Stalking or following
  • Unwelcome photography or videography
